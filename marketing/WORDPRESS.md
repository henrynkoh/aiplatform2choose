## The Problem: Information Overload and Analysis Paralysis

### The Exploding No-Code Ecosystem

### Common Challenges

When selecting no-code tools, builders face several critical challenges:

**1. Information Overload**
There are simply too many tools to research comprehensively. Each tool has multiple pricing tiers, feature sets, and use cases. Trying to compare them all manually is nearly impossible.

**2. Hidden Costs**
Many tools offer free tiers that seem perfect until you realize you need premium features. The actual cost can be 2-3x what you initially budgeted, derailing your project finances.

**3. Time Investment**
Researching tools takes 10-20 hours on average. That's time you could be spending actually building your project, testing your idea, or serving customers.

**4. Uncertainty**
Even after extensive research, you're often left wondering if you made the right choice. What if there's a better tool you didn't discover? What if the tool you chose doesn't scale with your needs?

**5. Analysis Paralysis**
With so many options, it's easy to get stuck in research mode and never actually start building. This paralysis prevents many great ideas from becoming reality.
