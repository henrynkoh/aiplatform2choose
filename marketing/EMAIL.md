## üìä Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use numbers and emojis sparingly
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Personalize when possible
- A/B test different variations

### Preheaders
- Complement subject line
- Add value or context
- Keep under 90 characters
- Use as second chance to engage

### Body Content
- Clear hierarchy
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- One main CTA
- Mobile-responsive design

### Timing
- Welcome: Immediate
- Onboarding: Day 1, 3, 7
- Weekly: Same day/time
- Re-engagement: After 30 days inactive
- Best send times: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM

### Personalization
- Use recipient's name
- Reference past behavior
- Segment by interests
- Dynamic content based on data

### Testing
- A/B test subject lines
- Test send times
- Test CTAs
- Test content length
- Monitor open and click rates
